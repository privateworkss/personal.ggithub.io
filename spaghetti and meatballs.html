Spaghetti and Meatballs

A classic Italian-American dish featuring savory meatballs served over spaghetti pasta and topped with rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 pound (450g) spaghetti pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, beaten egg, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and chopped parsley. Mix until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain well.
  5. Heat marinara sauce in a separate saucepan until warmed through.
  6. To serve, place cooked spaghetti on plates, top with meatballs, spoon marinara sauce over the meatballs, and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  7. Enjoy your delicious spaghetti and meatballs!
